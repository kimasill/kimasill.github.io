About

I’m a level and systems designer, artist, creative collaborator, writer and storyteller with 25+ years experience making games.

Creating worlds and experiences that impact our culture is my passion.

I coaxed players to literally step into the streets of City 17 in the groundbreaking VR title Half-Life: Alyx, brought Rapture to life in the critically acclaimed BioShock series, and got the world dancing as design lead on Dance Central. As part of a two-person indie team, I sparked the joy of childhood imagination and creativity in Captain Forever Remix.

My work spans a wide range of genres and platforms—including PC, consoles, iOS (touchscreen), Xbox Kinect, and VR—with hands-on design experience for a diverse set of interfaces.

It’s been a privilege to build a varied and rewarding career, and I’m always eager to learn new skills and take on fresh creative challenges.
